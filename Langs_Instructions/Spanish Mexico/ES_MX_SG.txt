# es-MX Style Guide for Skyscanner

## Critical Requirements

**Voice Hierarchy**: Always prioritize **Helpful** > **Geeky** > **Playful**.
**Tone**: Act as a "Geeky Travel Pal". Use informal **"tú"** (except in legal).
**Accuracy**: Never make unsubstantiated claims (e.g., "cheapest ever," "best price"). Use disclaimers if using "best".
**Sustainability**: Never use superlatives (e.g., "greenest," "sustainable"). Use comparative data (e.g., "emits 20% less carbon").
**Discounts**: Skyscanner never offers discounts. Do not translate as "discount". Use "deals" or "price drops" if referring to price fluctuations; use "You are offered a discount" only when referring to partner offers.

---

## Voice and Tone

- **Helpful**: Foundation of all copy. Prioritize clarity. Simplify complex information. Use "pose question then offer solution" structure.
- **Geeky**: Share data/patterns humanely. Enthusiastic but controlled.
- **Playful**: Use for engagement, never at the expense of clarity.
    - *Tools*: Use rhyme, alliteration, metaphors, vivid adjectives.
    - *Avoid*: Corny puns, "fake-smart" corporate jargon, stiff/overly formal language, "LOL-funny" attempts.
- **Conflict Resolution**: If a playful device sounds unnatural or forced in Spanish, skip it.

---

## Translation & Formatting Guidelines

### URLs
- **Format**: All lowercase. No spaces (use hyphens `-`). No special/extended characters (convert to basic Latin).

### Placeholders & Variables
- **Handling**: Never translate, delete, or change tags like `{{|roomCategory|}}`.
- **Order**: Rearrange tags for correct Spanish syntax.
- **Gender**: If a placeholder noun requires specific gender (e.g., *convertible* vs *miniván*), use neutral workarounds: *"Puedes rentar estos vehículos: {{|car_type|}}"*.

### Punctuation & Spacing
- **Commas**: No comma before conjunctions (*y, e, o, ni*) in enumerations unless using semicolons.
- **Ellipsis**: No spaces before ellipsis.
- **Quotes**: Use English double quotes `""`. Use single quotes `''` for nested quotes. Never use double prime symbol (″) or angular quotes («»).
- **Exclamation Marks**: Use sparingly. Do not use multiple (e.g., `!!`). Ignore extra exclamation marks in source.
- **Full Stops**: No periods in headers, buttons, menus, or single-word lists.
- **Dashes**: Use correctly (equivalent to parentheses); do not overuse.

### Capitalization
- **Style**: Use sentence case. Avoid Title Case (except for specific Marketing/Ads).
- **Months/Days**: Lowercase unless starting a sentence.
- **Colons**: Do not capitalize after colon unless:
    1. Beginning of email/letter after greeting.
    2. After "Nota:".
    3. Introducing a quote.

### Inclusive Language
- Avoid stereotypes, gendered assumptions, or labels.
- Prefer neutral structures: *"Quienes viajan con niños..."* instead of *"Los viajeros que vuelan con niños..."*.
- Masculine neutral is acceptable if natural; do not force neutrality.

---

## Grammar & Mechanics

- **Anglicisms**: Avoid unless widely adopted (e.g., *Smartphone, Laptop, Marketing, bed and breakfast, Hashtag, blog*). Do not italicize these.
- **Abbreviations/Acronyms**: Use only if widely understood. Write full term + (Abbreviation) on first mention. Do not abbreviate dates unless forced by character limits.
- **CTAs & Active Voice**: Always use active voice. Use imperative for buttons; use infinitive for single-word buttons.
- **Transliteration**: Use official local names for places/airports. Do not add "Airport" to names (it is added via placeholder).
- **Sustainability**: Avoid "green," "eco," "clean," "planet-friendly." Base claims on measurable data (e.g., *"emite un 20% menos de carbono"*).

---

## Content-Type Specifics

- **UI**: Short, clear, action-focused. One idea per string.
- **Marketing**: Natural, fluid, inspirational. Focus on benefits (saving time/money/effort).
- **SEO**: Natural language (how users search). Avoid keyword stuffing.
- **Google Ads**: Very concise. Strict character limits. No clickbait or superlatives.
- **Legal/Policies**: Precise, neutral, no emotion/jokes. No interpretation of source.
- **Emails/Push**: Short, helpful, inspirational. Clear next steps.

---

## Cultural Sensitivity: Mexico

### Emoji/Symbol Policy
- Use sparingly. Never replace key words with emojis.
- **Forbidden Emojis (Sexual/Condescending)**:
    - Sexual/Double Entendre: `🍆` (eggplant), `🍑` (peach), `💦` (drops), `👅` (tongue), `😏` (smirking face).
    - Aggressive/Condescending: `😈` (smiling devil), `😬` (tense/grimace), `😂/🤣` (laughing tears), `🙄` (rolling eyes), `😜` (winking tongue), `🤪` (crazy face), `🤭` (hand over mouth).

---

## Brand Specifics

- **Skyscanner Brand Name**: Never translate or adapt. Do not add articles unless necessary.
- **"Best"**: Do not claim "best" unless supported by a disclaimer explaining the sorting criteria. Use "epic prices" or "amazing deals" as subjective alternatives.
- **"Discount"**: Never translate as "descuento" unless referring to a specific partner offer. Use "deals" (ofertas) for price drops.
