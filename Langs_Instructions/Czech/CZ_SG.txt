# Czech Style Guide for Skyscanner

## Critical Requirements: Tone of Voice
**Primary Mission**: Be a "Geeky Travel Pal."
**Hierarchy**: If forced to choose, prioritize **Helpful** > **Geeky** > **Playful**.

- **Playful**: Use a conversational, lively, natural rhythm. Turn frustrations into tips/hacks. Use rhyme, alliteration, and metaphors ONLY if they feel natural. If they sound corny or forced, omit them.
- **Geeky**: Show expertise by breaking complex data into simple, human steps. Keep enthusiasm controlled (knowledgeable friend, not a lecturer).
- **Helpful**: Clear, honest, and proactive.
    - **Bad News**: Drop playfulness. Apologize sincerely once. Focus on solutions. Do not use cold phrases like "We apologize for the inconvenience."
    - **Clarity**: Put the traveler first. Ask nicely. Use "pose question then offer solution" format.

## URL Requirements
- **Format**: Lowercase, no spaces, Latin characters only.
- **Separators**: Use hyphens (`-`).
- **Special Characters**: Replace with basic Latin (e.g., replace `á` with `a`).

## Placeholders and Variables
- **Rule**: Do not translate, delete, or change placeholders/tags (e.g., `@@city@@`).
- **Grammar**: Keep exact source order. If Czech inflection causes grammatical errors, rephrase the surrounding sentence (e.g., use a neutral construction) to accommodate the variable without modifying it.
- **Spacing**: Ensure spaces exist before/after tags to prevent text gluing.

## Grammar and Punctuation
- **Source Reference**: Use [Czech Academy of Sciences](http://prirucka.ujc.cas.cz/) for all punctuation/spelling queries.
- **Quotation Marks**: Use Czech style: `„` (alt+0132) and `“` (alt+0147).
- **Ellipsis**: Use `…` (character) instead of `...` (three dots). Use sparingly.
- **Exclamation Marks**: Remove most. Use only for genuine enthusiasm or strong marketing. Never use in commanding/aggressive contexts (e.g., "Vyplňte dotazník!").
- **Hyphens vs. Dashes**:
    - **Hyphen (`-`)**: Morphological use (e-mail).
    - **En dash (`–`, alt+0150)**: Sentence level separation.
- **Semicolons**: Avoid. Split sentences or use conjunctions instead.
- **Non-breaking spaces (NBS)**: Required for:
    - Number + unit (20 km, 5 €)
    - Before percent sign (15 %)
    - Day + month (12. května)
    - Time + abbreviation (10 h)
    - After single-letter prepositions (v, s, k, z, etc.)

## Formatting and Capitalization
- **Case**: Use **sentence case** for titles, CTAs, and headers. Never use Title Case.
- **Months/Days**: Lowercase unless at the start of a sentence or proper name.
- **Dates**: Format: DD. MM. YYYY (e.g., 12. 5. 2025). No dot on the year.
- **Colons**: Lowercase after colon if explaining/listing; uppercase only if starting a new standalone sentence.
- **Product Features**: Use sentence case. Only capitalize official brand names.

## Inclusive and Neutral Language
- **Inclusivity**: Avoid stereotypes (age, gender, family, ability). Focus on traveler needs.
- **Gender**: Avoid gendered forms. If unavoidable, use forward slash (e.g., `Rezervoval/a jste si let`), but prioritize neutral rephrasing (e.g., "Let máte rezervovaný").

## Addressing the User
- **Formality**: Use formal "V-form" but keep it friendly, natural, and accessible. Avoid archaic or bureaucratic phrasing.
- **Active Voice**: Mandatory for CTAs. Use imperative mood (e.g., *Klikněte*, *Zaregistrujte se*).

## Terminology and Brand
- **Skyscanner**: Never translate. Inflect lightly only if natural.
- **Anglicisms**: Use Czech equivalents if common. Keep English terms only if standard in the travel industry (e.g., brand names).
- **Best/Superlatives**: Avoid objective superlatives ("nejlepší," "nejlevnější") unless providing a clear disclaimer/methodology. Use subjective terms ("skvělé ceny," "báječné nabídky") instead.
- **Discounts**: Skyscanner does not offer discounts; partners do. Use neutral phrasing: "You are offered a discount." Never confuse "deals" or "price drops" with "discounts."
- **Sustainability**: Avoid greenwashing. Use comparatives ("X is more sustainable than Y"). Do not use absolutes ("eco," "green," "zero emissions").

## Content-Type Guidelines
- **UI**: Short, clear, action-focused. 1 idea per string.
- **Marketing**: Natural, fluid, inspirational.
- **SEO**: Natural language (no keyword stuffing).
- **Google Ads**: Concise, benefit-driven, no clickbait.
- **App Store**: Benefits and features only; no superlatives.
- **Legal/Policies**: Neutral, precise, no emotion.

## Examples and Best Practices (Summary)

### Punctuation & Style
- **Do**: "Konečně způsob, jak porovnat lety..."
- **Don't**: "Připoutejte se, úspory startují!!" (Aggressive)
- **Do**: "Upozornění na cenu"
- **Don't**: "Cenové upozornění" (Be consistent).

### Sustainability (Don't Greenwash)
- **Do**: "Tento let produkuje o 20 % méně emisí než typický let na této trase."
- **Don't**: "Tento let je nejudržitelnější / nejzelenější / eko."

### CTAs and Buttons
- **Do**: "Zobrazit lety"
- **Don't**: "Zobrazení výhodných letových nabídek" (Avoid noun strings, use active imperatives).

### Addressing the User
- **Do**: "Rezervujte hned teď"
- **Don't**: "Rezervujte Hned Teď!" (Sentence case, no exclamation abuse).

### Brand and Localization
- **Do**: "Letiště Paris Charles de Gaulle"
- **Don't**: "Pařížské letiště Charles de Gaulle" (Retain official naming).
- **Do**: "Zjistěte více o Drops"
- **Don't**: "Funkce Drops, kterou od nás máte se slevou" (Never claim discounts).

### Formatting Exceptions
- **Do**: "12. 5. 2025"
- **Don't**: "12.05.2025" or "12. 5. 2025." (Correct: Day. Month Year).

### Placeholders
- **Source**: "Search flights to @@city@@"
- **Do**: "Vyhledat lety ve městě @@city@@" (Rephrased for grammatical flow).
